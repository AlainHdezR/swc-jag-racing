Computer-Aided Design (CAD)

Our CAD team used SolidWorks to design and create a 3D model of our vehicle. Everything from the sleek frame of the car to the smallest nuts and bolts has been precisely rendered.

Beyond the screen, our CAD team collaborates closely with other specialized teams. Collectively our CAD team has forged a comprehensive vision for our vehicle, where precision meets creativity.

Members: David Aragon, Gregory Fangon, Esteban Lopez, Melino Lopez, Oscar Sumaya.

Chasis

The chassis subteam is responsible for designing and constructing the fundamental structure that will securely house the driver and our vehicle's various components. Our dedicated team meticulously crafts the chassis to adhere to stringent safety standards and regulations set by Baja SAE.

Beyond the foundational framework, the chassis subteam collaborates seamlessly with our other specialized subteams to guarantee that every element harmoniously fits within the overall design. With a keen focus on agility, our team ensures that the chassis remains nimble, allowing for responsive maneuvering.

Members: Adrian Contreras, Yibel Gonzalez, Joel Kendrick, Arleth Lizarraga, Maria Tamayo.

Electrical

Our electrical subteam is tasked with designing and implementing crucial electrical systems within the vehicle. Such as the kill switches, brake signaling, and the data acquisition (DAQ).

The ultimate goal is to deliver a comprehensive and reliable electrical infrastructure that prioritizes safety, adheres to regulatory standards, and enhances the overall performance of our off-road vehicle in challenging terrains.

Members: Lance Brown, Marc Jones, Victoria Lopez-Villa
