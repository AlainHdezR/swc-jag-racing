About Us!

Jag Racing is a cutting-edge student led program designed to put classroom knowledge into practice by designing, fabricating, testing, and competing in automotive challenges. We believe that collaborative teamwork and hands-on experience is essential to the development of young engineers. Our program provides students with unique opportunities to learn and sharpen their skills, while also fostering a sense of community. Whether you are brand new to automotive design or have a passion for engineering, join us and discover the limitless potential of your ingenuity.

Our Car!

Our mission is to excel in the intricate process of developing a top-tier car that meets the stringent demands of design, manufacturing, and performance.
